MindSafe Chrome Extension

Supports healthier screen-time by analyzing YouTube videos and providing child-wellbeing insights directly in the browser. Designed for healthcare innovators, digital wellbeing projects, and parents seeking safer viewing experiences.

What it Does

  • Detects YouTube video changes & collects content signals
  • Generates wellbeing-focused safety scores
  • Highlights pacing, emotional tone, and age suitability
  • Displays results instantly inside the extension popup
  • Works on both YouTube and YouTube Kids
